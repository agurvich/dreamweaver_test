As of Yet Unnamed K12 Umbrella Program (AYUK12UP)

AYUK12UP is a CIERA outreach initiative that strives to connect K12 educators and their students to resources in astronomy. It is an umbrella program that has many facets. If there is something you or your class are interested in and would like support for we would be happy to help develop the resources and bring our expertise to your classroom.

AYUK12UP seeks to target schools with underserved populations in order to inspire the next generation of scientists and make astronomy a more diverse field. This is a core guiding principle in our work to bring approachable outreach materials to your classroom.

For students

Activities and opportunities for you

Download instructions for hands-on activities, participate in citizen science, or get answers to questions about space from real astronomers.

For teachers

Resources for your class

Download lesson plans, connect your class to astronomers, sign up your students for a science fiction club, or download posters to decorate your classroom .

For parents

Resources for you and your child

Download posters to decorate your child's room, sign up your child for a science fiction club, download instructions for hands-on activities for home.

For scientists

Resources to share with your community

Access materials to run your own outreach programs or contribute to our bank. Get connected with other astronomers who are invested in K12 outreach.
